Origins and Evolution of TechCon

TechConÂ® North America was first held in 1996 in New Orleans, Louisiana. It has since moved about the U.S., accommodating the growing number of delegates and utility hosts. It often includes a regional facility tour of the sponsoring utility. Most recently, the conference averaged 200 in attendance representing 11 various countries and 30 interactive exhibiting corporations. TechCon North America offers a vibrant and unparalleled learning experience with top-notch topics and speakers.

Goals and Driving Principles of the Conference

TechCon 2024 aims to foster innovation, collaboration, and knowledge sharing within the technology community. By bringing together industry leaders, researchers, and emerging talents, the conference seeks to explore cutting-edge technologies, discuss their potential impact, and inspire groundbreaking solutions. The event is driven by the principles of inclusivity, diversity, and ethical technology practices, fostering a vibrant and dynamic environment for learning and networking.

Notable Speakers from previous years

  1. Sundar Pichai, CEO of Alphabet and Google: As the head of one of the world's most influential tech companies, Pichai has overseen Google's expansion into various domains, including search, AI, and self-driving cars. His vision has shaped the digital landscape and driven innovation.
  2. Satya Nadella, CEO of Microsoft: Nadella has transformed Microsoft into a cloud-first, mobile-first company. His leadership has been instrumental in the success of products like Azure and Office 365, positioning Microsoft as a major player in the tech industry.
  3. Susan Wojcicki, former CEO of YouTube: Wojcicki played a pivotal role in the growth of YouTube, transforming it into a global video-sharing platform. Her leadership and strategic vision have shaped the way we consume and create online video content.
